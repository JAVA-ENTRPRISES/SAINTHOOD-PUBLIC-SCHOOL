Learning design

Academy

How we sequence lessons, stretch able learners, and support the ones who need a little more time— all in an English-medium environment.

Curriculum spine

Core subjects follow board-aligned outcomes with weekly milestones. Teachers share “learning intentions” at the start of lessons so students know what success looks like.

  • English, mathematics, science, social studies
  • Second language and reading blocks
  • Digital literacy where it deepens understanding

Faculty

Subject specialists and primary homeroom mentors combine—regular training keeps methods fresh.

Progress

Formative checks, projects, and term exams paint a full picture—not a single snapshot.

Infrastructure

Ventilated classrooms, lab blocks, library periods, and quiet corners for small-group reading— spaces shaped around concentration, not decoration alone.

Signature experiences

Short programmes that repeat every year—students anticipate them, parents see growth.

Speaker’s corner

Weekly stage time for every class—from nursery rhymes to structured debates in senior grades.

Lab days

Science isn’t a chapter to memorise; it’s a hypothesis to test, record, and discuss in English.

House championships

Sport, quiz, and art points build house spirit and teach graceful winning—and losing.
